About the College
Welcome to Kaloleni Technical and Vocational College, a government owned technical institution providing technical education and training under the Ministry of Education, State Department of Technical, Vocational Education and Training (TVET). The college was established in 2019 under the mentorship of Kenya Coast National Polytechnic (KCNP) and we are proud to be associated with them. We are located at Birini/Mwamleka Sub-location of Chanagande location in Kaloleni Constituency, Kilifi County, Kenya. We offer market driven courses under four (4) academic departments; - Mechanical and Automotive Engineering, Computing and Informatics (ICT), Hospitality and Institutional Management, and Electrical & Electronics Engineering. Believing in skills development for a bright future, we make every life to matter by imparting useful skills through a team of highly qualified and competent staff thanks to the government and the Public Service Commission. Since the college inception, with the support of our Board of Governors, we have made a great positive difference in technical training by providing the market with skilled manpower to change the world socially and economically. Inclusivity and resilience are tightly sandwiched in our institutional culture hence making this transformation possible.We have progressively become the preferred agent of change that will transform the highly industrializing economy and national development.
